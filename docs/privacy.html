Privacy Policy — Poomsae App

Last updated: April 2026

No personal data collected

The Poomsae app developed by Aeon Shaker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No account, no sign-up

The app does not require any user account, registration, or login.

Local-only preferences

All your settings (selected language, view counter, donation prompt status) are stored only on your device using Android's standard local storage (SharedPreferences) and never leave your phone.

No analytics, no trackers

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies.

YouTube videos

The app embeds YouTube videos for Poomsae demonstrations. When you play a video, YouTube may collect data according to Google's privacy policy. The Poomsae app does not control or access this data.

Donation links

If you choose to donate, you will be redirected to external services (Ko-fi, PayPal). These platforms apply their own privacy policies. We do not receive any personal information from these services.

Permissions

The only Android permission requested by the app is INTERNET, used exclusively to load YouTube videos.

Children

The app is suitable for all audiences and does not target users under 13. No personal data is collected from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL. Please check this page from time to time.

Contact

For any question regarding privacy, please contact: aeonshakerv@gmail.com

Letzte Aktualisierung: April 2026

Die von Aeon Shaker entwickelte Poomsae-App erhebt, speichert oder übermittelt keine personenbezogenen Daten.

Die einzige von der App angeforderte Android-Berechtigung ist INTERNET, die ausschließlich zum Laden von YouTube-Videos verwendet wird.

Die App ist für alle Zielgruppen geeignet und richtet sich nicht an Nutzer unter 13 Jahren. Es werden keine personenbezogenen Daten von Nutzern erhoben, unabhängig von ihrem Alter.
